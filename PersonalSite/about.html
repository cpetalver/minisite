About Me

As you saw on the previous page, my name is Cyan Petalver. If you want to know more about me, then you've come to the right page! I was born in Manilla, Philippines before lunch (my mom likes to joke that I was born then because I didn't want to be late for lunch). I quickly moved to the United States when I was just 2 years old, so even though I have tried to reconnect with my Filipino heritage, I would say that for the most part I am American. I moved around a lot when I was little which made it hard growing up as far as making friends and figuring out my identity. It was okay though, because the one constant in my life was art and video games.
