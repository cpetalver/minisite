Cyan's Personal Site

Hi there! My name is Cyan Petalver. I am 22 years old, a student at San Francisco State University, and I am studying Visual Communication Design. I am highly interested in many fields related to graphic design, button design related to motion intrigues me the most. I have always had a fondness for all the arts from my love of playing the piano, my unabashed desire to paint and draw every day, and my past as a color guard performer. So, it's safe to say that I have always been invested in the arts, and whatever your opinion on graphic design as art may be, I wanted to carry out this passion in the professional field.
